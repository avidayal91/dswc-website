SCMIP TECHNICAL DETAILS

TECHNICAL DETAILS LIST

Project Detail
Implementation Entity Department of Soil and Water Conservation, Govt of Punjab, India
Execution Entity Divisional Soil Conservation Officer, Hoshiarpur
Project Status Operational
Cost of Project 40.94 cr
Command Area 664 hectares.
Funding Entities Govt of Punjab, Supported by NABARD from under RIDF-18 and Rastriya Krishi Vikas Yogna(RKVY), GoI
Project Owners The Community of Project Area
Main Benefits Asured Irrigation, Energy Conservaion, Water Conservation, Protection from Wild Animals
Water to be Utilized/Lifted 15.7 cusecs
Source of Water Kandi Canal
Number of Villages to be benefitted 14 nos
Villages to be benefited Repur, Depur, Badala, Passi Karora, Kando Karora, Narnaul, Gaghwal, Paralian Chak, Gaggar, Asafpur, Badalian, Jugial, Bharath, Swar
Number of Households 810 nos
Total number of Population to be benefitted 8000 nos
Total number of farmers to be benefitted 1200 nos
Source of Energy Exclusively Solar Power (Solar Photovoltaic Systems)
Type of Irrigation Systems Deployed Drip and Sprinkler Irrigation Systems
Mode of Operation Remotely Operated Fully Computerized Automated System
Total Number of Solar Photovoltaic Panels(SPV) Installed 3798 nos
Peak Energy Generation Capacity of SPV system 1.1 MW
Total Number and Capacity of Pumpsets Deployed 46 nos pumpsets of 15-23 hp capacity.
Type of Pipeline Laid High Denesity Polyethylene(HDPE) and Poly Vinyl Chloride (PVC)
Underground Pipeline Laid 7300 meters of HDPE pipeline and 60,000 meters of PVC pipeline.
Protection From Wild Animals 11 km Fencing upto 4 meters height has been installed to protect farm lands from wild animals.
Operation Mechanism Shall be Operated by 18 Water User Associations formed from amongst the beneficiary farmers.
Power Buy Back Mechanism Surplus power @100 days to be sold to State Power Department.
Project Operation & Maintenance Hand holding periodn -Department to operate and maintain project for seven years
Project Execution Contractor Jain Irrigation Systems Limited
